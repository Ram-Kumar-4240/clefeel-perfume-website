Our Story

Crafting fragrances that define elegance since 2010

The Beginning

A Passion for Perfumery

Clefeel was born from a deep passion for the art of perfumery. Founded in 2010 by master perfumer Arjun Mehta, our journey began in a small atelier in Mumbai with a simple mission: to create fragrances that capture emotions, memories, and moments in time.

What started as a humble workshop has grown into a globally recognized luxury brand, yet our commitment to craftsmanship and quality remains unchanged. Each Clefeel fragrance is a testament to our dedication to the art of scent.
